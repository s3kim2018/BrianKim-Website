A Little About Me...

Hello! I'm Brian, a second-year Computer Science student at the University of California, Berkeley. I am also a lab assistant for CS61B, a data structures class, a project developer for the Daily Californian, and a web dev assistant for UC Berkeley's Diversity and Inclusion division

I was born in Korea and grew up at Saskatoon SK Canada, and I came to the United States for high school. Other than Computer Science, I love History and Political Science.

My favorite data structure is graphs and my favorite programming language is Python. I like how flexible graphs are and can create steroid versions of itself to other data structures (like Trees!). I love Python because I like to use both procedural and object-oriented designs when programming.
